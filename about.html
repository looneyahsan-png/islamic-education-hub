السلام عليكم ورحمة الله وبركاته

My name is Ahsan, a 14-year-old student from Pakistan 🇵🇰. I've been passionate about technology and Islamic knowledge since childhood. When I started programming at age 12, I dreamed of using my skills to serve the Muslim community.

Islamic Education Hub is my humble contribution to Sadaqah Jariyah - continuous charity that benefits Muslims worldwide. Every line of code was written with sincere intention and love for Allah SWT and our Ummah.

I believe that technology can be a powerful tool to spread Islamic knowledge and connect Muslims around the world. This platform is designed to make authentic Islamic resources easily accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background.

I'm always open to feedback and collaboration. Feel free to reach out through my social media!

May Allah accept this effort and make it beneficial for all Muslims. Ameen.
